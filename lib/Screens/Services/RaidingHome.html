Raids and Strategies

Raiding is a big part of Final Fantasy. It comes in many difficulties, both for casuals and high end raiders. The many challenges that the game offers are a great way to test your skills and teamwork.

Normal

Normal Raids are the casual tier of raiding. Most of them you simply come accross while doing the main story and apart from a few, all are easily clearable by most players and dont need setup time to do

Savage

Savage Raids are the mid tier of raiding. They are harder than normal raids and require a bit more coordination and skill to clear. They are a great way to test your skills and teamwork

Ultimate

Ultimate Raids are the high end tier of raiding. They are the hardest content in the game and require a lot of coordination and skill to clear. They are a great way to test your skills and teamwork
