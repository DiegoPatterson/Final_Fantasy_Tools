Tank Classes

Welcome to the Tank page. Here you can find a list of Tank Services we offer.

Dark Knight

Dark Knight is the Edgy magic based tank. It has one of the highest burst windows out of all the tanks and the highest magic mitigation in the game. It doesnt do well into physical damage bosses, but when against a magic damage boss, it is able to shine.


Gun Breaker

Gun Breaker is the physical damage tank. It makes use of a parry mechanic that can nullify incoming damage from physical based attacks. With a large burst window, this tank excels in its select fields.


Paladin

Paladin is the classic tank that most people think of when thinking tank. It carrys a big shild that allows it to natrually have more starting mitigation from any other tank. Likewise, It also has many healing mechanics and gaurding mechanics to protect and keep your team alive. If you want to be a team player and still have good general mitigation, this is the way to go.


Warrior

Warrior is the high health, high damage, high healing tank. Despite having many mitigation tools in its kit, the warrior is known for making using of self healing abilities to keep itself alive. Often times, this tank can do well on its own even without a healer present. Like wise, it has a massive damage output and easy rotation. If you want to be a tank that can do it all, this is the tank for you.
